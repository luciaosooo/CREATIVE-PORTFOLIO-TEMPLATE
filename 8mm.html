8MM Magazine

8mm is a collectible cinema magazine that dedicates each curated issue to a single film director. For the first issue, we chose to celebrate Pedro Almodóvar. His bold and distinctive aesthetic is reflected across the magazine, through the use of vibrant colors, Sans Serif typography for the titles, and creative layouts that blend photography with illustration. To maintain balance, some spreads feature bold, colorful backgrounds, while others use clean, white spaces. When creating the illustrations I decided they shouldn’t have a lot of volume, so they would fit with the bold colors in the magazine and the collage style, which is very important in Almodóvar’s aesthetic.
