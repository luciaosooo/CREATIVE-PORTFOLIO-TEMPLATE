About me

I'm a Graphic Design and Multimedia student based in Madrid, passionate about creating visual identities that tell meaningful stories.

With a focus on minimalist aesthetics and strategic thinking, I specialize in branding, editorial design, and user experience. My work combines clean design principles with thoughtful attention to detail.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me exploring Madrid's art galleries, experimenting with analog photography, or searching for the perfect café con leche.
