Nintendo Window Display

This project explores the design of a commercial window display inspired by Animal Crossing: New Leaf, using the Nintendo Switch as an immersive portal between the physical and digital worlds. The scene recreates a cozy in-game environment, translating the game’s visual language into a three-dimensional retail showcase. Special attention was given to color accuracy and lighting, ensuring the palette and overall illumination closely match the in-game appearance. The result is an inviting and recognizable display that communicates the game’s atmosphere while highlighting the console as the central product.
