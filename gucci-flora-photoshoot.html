Gucci Flora Photoshoot

This Gucci Flora campaign focuses on the connection between fragrance and memory. Flowers are used as a visual metaphor for both the scent and the emotions tied to it, creating a soft, nostalgic mood. The lighting and color palette stay gentle and airy to reflect the delicate nature of the fragrance. The product is placed within floral elements to make it feel personal and almost dreamlike, rather than purely commercial. By including a portrait, the campaign adds a human touch, suggesting that Gucci Flora is not just a perfume, but an experience tied to personal moments and feelings.
