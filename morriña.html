Morriña

Morriña is a concept for a premium Galician food export platform that blends tradition with modern design. The website emphasizes storytelling, cultural identity, and user experience, featuring clean visuals, intuitive navigation, and value-added sections such as recipe ideas. Beyond the website, I developed a full visual identity,creating branded products like: sardine tins, octopus packaging, and other regional specialties. The project captures the essence of Galicia through clean visuals, cultural storytelling, and a cohesive brand experience, offering an authentic yet contemporary take on regional gastronomy.
