Starbucks Seasons

This campaign consists of three illustrations that represent different seasons and festivities such as: Christmas, Valentine’s Day and summer. It is designed to reflect the vibrant spirit of New York City. Each piece celebrates the city’s creative energy while positioning Starbucks as a place of pause and community. With colorful palettes, fluid lines, and a lively rhythm, the visuals capture the movement of urban life and invite audiences to connect emotionally with the brand.
