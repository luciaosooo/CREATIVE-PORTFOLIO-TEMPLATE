Sitges Film Festival

I redesigned the brand identity of the Sitges Film Festival’s, creating a bold and modern visual identity centered on a striking gorilla icon. Beyond the logo, I developed a comprehensive brand refresh, including stationery design, social media, digital advertising assets such as banners, and event materials like step-and-repeat backdrops for the red carpet featuring sponsors. This cohesive rebranding ensured a consistent and impactful visual presence across both print and digital platforms.
